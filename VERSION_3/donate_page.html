DONATE BOOKS FOR KIDS

Thank you for your interest in our Donation Books Program! We accept donations of gently used books for all ages—children's books, textbooks, novels, and educational materials. Your donations will go to:
📖 Underprivileged people
📚 Community learning centers
📘 Literacy advocacy groups

✅ How to Donate:
Check that your books are in good condition (no missing pages or heavy damage).
Pack them securely in a box.
Drop them off at our main office or schedule a pickup.
📅 We accept donations every Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
